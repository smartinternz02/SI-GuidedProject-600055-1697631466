Disease Prediction using Machine Learning

In today's fast-paced world, finding time for healthcare can be challenging. Often, even with severe symptoms, people hesitate to visit a doctor. Turning to Google for symptom searches often leads to generic results, providing only stereotypical disease suggestions. As a result, many individuals have grown reluctant to use Google for self-diagnosis. Addressing these issues, we've developed a model capable of predicting up to 42 diseases based on input symptoms. This model serves as a valuable tool for both doctors and patients. Doctors can use it for online consultations, while patients can leverage it for preventive diagnosis and self-care, especially considering the high costs associated with in-person doctor visits. Notably, our model prioritizes user privacy by not requiring personal data such as name, age, gender, religion, or address. Feel free to use this web application whenever you suspect you might be experiencing symptoms of a disease. The model will provide probable disease suggestions based on the symptoms, empowering you to make informed decisions about whether to seek professional medical advice.
